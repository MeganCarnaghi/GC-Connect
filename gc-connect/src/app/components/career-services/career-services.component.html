Graduation Requirements

Below is an outline of the Career Services requirements every job-seeking student must complete in order to be eligible for graduation. Any student who has not met those requirements by the start of Final Projects will go through a Career Services review and may be removed from bootcamp.

Requirement Description Relevant Links Due Date
Attend all Soft Skills sessions There will be a total of 6 mandatory Soft Skills sessions. These sessions will be held during classtime. Not Applicable See GC's Calendar for upcoming Soft Skills session dates.
Participate in two 1:1 Coaching Sessions During your bootcamp, you are required to meet with a member of the career services team at least TWICE. You are always welcome to schedule more appointments with us! Your first coaching session needs to happen within the first 3 weeks of bootcamp. Use a Career Services team member's Calendly link above to schedule a 1:1 coaching session. Prior to your last bootcamp class.
Update your LinkedIn profile Using the rubric provided, update your LinkedIn profile to meet the requirements. You can find the rubric here. Check the GC Calendar to see when this assignment is due for your cohort.
Update your resume Using the rubric provided, update your resume to meet the requirements. You can find the rubric here. Check the GC Calendar to see when this assignment is due for your cohort.
Participate in mock interviews Mock behavioral and technical interviews are an excellent way to prepare you for the real thing. The week prior to your mock interview, you will be sent a calendar invite including information about the company and role you are interviewing for. Not applicable Check the GC Calendar to see when mock interviews will be held for your cohort.
Participate in the final group project and submit it to the Student Portal Throughout bootcamp, you will be required to participate in three group projects. The final project will be submitted to the Student Portal for potential employers, Grand Circus staff and future students to explore. You can create an account for and browse the Student Portal here. Check the GC Calendar to see when this assignment is due for your cohort.
