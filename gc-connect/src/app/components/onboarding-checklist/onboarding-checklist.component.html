Welcome to GC-Connect!

Welcome to Grand Circus (GC) Connect! GC-Connect will serve as the main hub for all of your Bootcamp, Career Services, Affinity Group and Events information needs. Below you will find a checklist of items you need to complete prior to your first day of class.

Action Item Contact for Questions Completed
Set up your Slack account (check email for invite). support@grandcircus.co
Pay the remainder of your tuition. admissions@grandcircus.co
Complete the Getting to Know You Survey prior to orientation. admissions@grandcircus.co
Complete your GC-Connect profile (upload a picture, fill out your bio, add your LinkedIn and GitHub url's, etc.) Not applicable
Complete all intro exercises in the LMS. instructor@grandcircus.co
Bookmark your class's Zoom link. You'll be using this link 3x a week. Having it bookmarked in your browser allows you to find it quickly! instructor@grandcircus.co
Review the GC calendar. Make note of any important dates (orientation, first day of class, soft skills sessions, etc.) admissions@grandcircus.co
Explore the Career Services Page. Learn about the CS team members, review the requirements for graduation, make note of the upcoming Soft Skills session dates and assignment due dates, begin scheduling your 1:1 sessions, etc. careerservices@grandcircus.co
Explore the GC Affinity Groups. Join any of the groups that are of interest to you or create a new group! Not applicable
